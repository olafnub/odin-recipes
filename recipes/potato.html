Potato Recipe | Ultimate Meal Prep Guide

Author by Linley Hanson Approx time: 1 Hour

Welcome to the guide of making potatoes for your next Meal Prep

Look at Required materials below

  • Red potatoes cut into smaller sizes
  • Onions (but not necessary, just for extra flavor)
  • Olive oil - to make it nice and crispy
  • Spices needed
    • salt
    • pepper
    • garlic powder
    • dired oregano
    • chili powder

Instructions, Step by Step Guide

  1. Wash chunk potatoes and slice them
  2. Place slices potatoes on baking sheet
  3. Mix seasoning and coat potatoes, also with with olive oil
  4. Mix the potatoes together until all are evenly coated
  5. Roast at 425 Fahrenheit for 30-40 minutes, in between 15 minutes, make sure to flip potatoes
