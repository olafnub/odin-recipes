Chicken Breast Recipe | Ultimate Meal Prep Guide

Meal prepping with Chicken Breast -- Author is Kate Jones and the Prep time is 15 Minutes, Brine time is 1 hour and Cook time is 20 Minutes

SEO: Recipe for chicken breast, meal prepping and air fry

Brine -- 1 Hour
  • 1 lime
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
Chicken -- 35 minutes
  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
Step by Step
  1. Soak chicken with mixed onion & garlic powder and lime for 1 hour
  2. Mix seasoning and massage chicken breast
  3. Place chicken breast in air fryer
  4. Cook for 9 minutes at 360 fahrenheit
  5. Flip the chicken and cook for 9 miunutes again

    6. Enjoy! Can be refrigerated for 7 days
